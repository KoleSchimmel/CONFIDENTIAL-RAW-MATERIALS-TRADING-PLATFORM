PROJECT SUMMARY
===============

This is a complete, production-grade implementation of a Fully Homomorphic
Encryption (FHE) based marketplace for raw materials trading on Ethereum
blockchain. It demonstrates advanced FHE concepts in a real-world B2B context
while maintaining complete privacy of sensitive business information.
